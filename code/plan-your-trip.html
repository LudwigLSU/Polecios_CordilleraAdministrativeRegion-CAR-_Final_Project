Travel Tips

  • Book accommodations in advance, especially during holidays.
  • Bring light jackets, the mountain air can be chilly.
  • Respect local traditions and communities.
  • Bring cash, as ATMs may not be available in remote areas.

Packing Checklist

  • Comfortable walking shoes
  • Reusable water bottle
  • Rain jacket or umbrella
  • Power bank and phone charger
  • Camera or smartphone for photos
