Tourist Spots

Baguio city, Benguet: Burnham Park

A well-known park located in the heart of Baguio City. It's surrounded by flowers, pine trees, and a man-made lake where tourists can go boating.

Location: Banaue, Ifugao Province


Sagada Hanging Coffins & Echo Valley (Mountain Province)

n the Mountain Province, Sagada is famous for its ancient custom of placing coffins on cliffs, and for its scenic trails in Echo Valley

Location: Sagada, Mountain Province.


Banaue Rice Terraces (Ifugao)

These ancient rice terraces carved into the mountains of Ifugao are often called the “Eighth Wonder of the World.” They offer spectacular views and a glimpse into the ingenious farming methods of the highland people.

Location: Banaue, Ifugao Province


Tam-awan Village (Baguio City, Benguet)

A cultural village in Baguio that showcases traditional highland huts, art by indigenous artists, and immersive experiences in the local heritage.

Location: Baguio City, Benguet Province.
