Destinations History Activities Plan your trip

Adventure

Experience the thrill of the Cordilleras through exciting outdoor activities surrounded by breathtaking mountain landscapes.

  • Hiking to Mount Pulag for the famous "sea of clouds"
  • Boating at Burnham Park in Baguio City
  • Rock Climing and Spelunking in Sagada's caves
  • Mountain biking through Benguet's scenic trails
  • Camping in the cool weather of Atok, Benguet

Culture

Discover the living traditions, artistry, and history of the Cordilleran people.

  • Visit Tam-awan Village to experience Cordilleran art and heritage
  • Watch traditional dances during Panagbenga Festival
  • Learn traditional weaving in Ifugao or Abra
  • Attend cultural tours and storytelling by indigenous elders
  • Explore Sagada's hanging coffins and Ifugaoâ€™s ancient rice terraces

Foods

Treat yourself to unique mountain flavors and cozy Baguio treats.

  • Go strawberry picking in La Trinidad, Benguet
  • Try Benguet coffee at local cafes
  • Taste camote bread or ube jam from Good Shepherd Convent
  • Enjoy pinikpikan, a traditional Cordilleran dish
  • Visit the Baguio Public Market for local produce and delicacies
